# Product

## Register

product

## Users

ClaimBot is for consumers and client accounts that want a simple way to review possible class-action settlement opportunities, save relevant profile facts, and track claim progress. Users may not understand legal or technical setup details, so the normal product surface should keep the workflow clear: set up profile, review matches, track claims.

## Product Purpose

ClaimBot compares saved facts against settlement data, helps users review potential matches, and supports guarded automation for paid accounts when a no-proof claim clears permission, plan, form, and safety checks. Success means customers can understand the next action without reading launch paperwork, while the product still avoids promising eligibility, payout certainty, or proof bypasses.

## Brand Personality

Plainspoken, careful, capable.

## Anti-references

Avoid launch-console language on customer screens, legal certainty claims, payout hype, dense admin dashboards, raw command snippets, and setup artifacts outside Launch, Packet Center, Audit, and Settings. Avoid making paid automation feel like a semi-automated checklist for eligible no-proof claims.

## Design Principles

- One next action beats many status panels.
- Customer pages explain outcomes and limits, not implementation details.
- Paid automation is hands-off only when the guardrails are truly clear.
- Safety language should be specific and short.
- Operator evidence belongs in Launch and Packet Center, not the main customer flow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Keep touch targets at least 44px, preserve visible focus states, avoid color-only status communication, respect reduced motion, and write copy that works for users without legal, technical, or claims-administration knowledge.
